About Serima High School

Serima High School is a Catholic mission institution in Zimbabwe, dedicated to nurturing minds, building character, and fostering faith. We provide a safe and supportive environment for students to thrive academically and spiritually.

Admission Requirements
  • Completed application form
  • Previous school report or results
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Recommendation from previous school (if applicable)
How to Apply

Complete the online application form or download the PDF application. Submit it to the school office along with required documents. Our admissions team will contact you for further steps.

Our Academic Programs & School Activities

Serima High School nurtures academic excellence, spiritual growth, and holistic development through our wide range of subjects, practical projects, and vibrant extracurricular activities.

English Language & Literature

Strengthen reading, writing, and comprehension skills at both O-Level and A-Level.

Mathematics

Develop logical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical skills through structured lessons.

Physics, Chemistry & Biology

Engage in hands-on experiments and theory classes to master the sciences at both levels.

Shona Language & Culture

Learn Zimbabwean language, literature, and cultural heritage as part of holistic education.

Religious Studies & Catholic Formation

Deepen understanding of the Catholic faith through scripture, moral education, and spiritual life.

Agriculture & Practical Projects

Hands-on learning with poultry, piggery, bakery, and crop cultivation projects for practical skills.

Art, Sculpture & Wood Carving

Develop creativity inspired by Serima Mission’s rich tradition in religious art and woodcarving.

Sports & Clubs

Participate in soccer, netball, volleyball, debate, chess, environmental, and drama clubs.
